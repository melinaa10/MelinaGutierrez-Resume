Melina Gutierrez

Interior Desinger

Over the course of my four year academic studies at the University of California Riverside, I developed a deep passion for interior design. Immediately after graduation, I travelled around the world, determined to obtain a better understanding of the vast array of cultures and their "aesthetic". This humbling opportunity has allowed me to successfully capture the lifestyle and beliefs my clients follow by simpling choosing the appropriate ornamentation via colors and patterns.

Of my 5 years of experience in this field, I have been graciously granted the opportunity to work alongside some of the world's most talented designers. I have collaborated on multiple occasions with brands such as Target, Croft House and Sunbeam Vintage and have also worked under individuals like Joanna Gaines, Bobby Berk and Justina Blakeney.

Whether it be your very first property or a secondary abode, my wish and duty is to make your dream home come to life.
